About Us

Charlesaaron Custom Solutions is a residential and commercial consumer electronic systems provider with nearly a decade worth of industry knowledge and experience. Since our inception, we have grown to become one of Nigeria's leading suppliers and installers of everything needed to make your home or office both technically and environmentally forward thinking. Our expertise spans across many specialties including smart home and office technologies, residential and commercial electrical services, security, audio and video distribution systems, residential and commercial cinemas, intelligent lighting and shading, video conferencing, design, consultancy services and more.

The wide breadth of our services have been provided to a wide scope of clients, both small and large, and have been integrated into hotels, corporate offices, entertainment venues, convention centers, sporting arenas, educational institutions, museums, retail environments, government buildings, public buildings, restaurants, residential buildings and various houses of worship.

We work hard to ensure that all of our solutions are forward thinking; consistently studying new markets, diligently analyzing existing technologies in order to uncover areas of improvement, and we are not afraid to take-on emerging technology in order to pioneer its introduction into the market. After all, that’s our team has been able to reshape existing industries.
